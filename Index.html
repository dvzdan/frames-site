Double Take Frames

Physical photos that change in the blink of an eye, according to a timer you set.

Gift a cherished photo and let it blend into the home. Then, a day, a week, or even a month later, the frame suddenly reveals a completely different image.

How it works

Two printed images sit inside one physical frame. A small timer-driven mechanism reveals the hidden image later.
